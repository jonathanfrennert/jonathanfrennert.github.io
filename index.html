Jonathan Gustafsson Frennert

I'm a PhD student at the University of Manchester, focusing on AI-driven design for soft robotics, supervised by Wei Pan, Charlie C.L. Wang, and Rika Antonova.

I completed my master's at the University of Edinburgh, researching self-supervised physical scene understanding with Subramanian Ramamoorthy. I also did an exchange at Caltech, researching thermal water segmentation for aerial vehicles with Soon-Jo Chung.

Research

I'm interested in problems at the intersection of robotics, computer vision and computer graphics. Most of my research has been about simulation-based learning of design, control and perception for autonomous systems.
